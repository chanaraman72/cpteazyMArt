About EAZYMART

Your local link to a better shopping experience.

Our Story

EAZYMART was born from a simple mission: to simplify grocery shopping for the community of Chilakaluripet.

We recognized that in our bustling town, convenience is key. Our goal is to connect you with the freshest produce and best products from your favorite local stores, all delivered right to your doorstep. As a modern delivery partner, we aim to support the local economy while making your daily life a little easier.

Founded by AQ & AR Enterprisers, EAZYMART is built on a foundation of reliability, quality, and a deep commitment to our local community. We believe that technology can be a powerful tool for good, and we're proud to use it to bring a world of convenience to our neighbors.

Our Mission & Values

We are driven by a commitment to local quality, community, and service excellence.

Community First

We partner with local businesses to ensure their products are easily accessible, fostering a stronger local economy.

Quality & Freshness

We guarantee that every item you order is hand-picked for its quality and freshness, just as if you were in the store yourself.

Reliable Service

Our dedicated team works hard to provide fast and dependable delivery, making your shopping experience stress-free.
