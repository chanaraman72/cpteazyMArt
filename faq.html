General Questions

EAZYMART is a local grocery and delivery service in Chilakaluripet, Guntur. We partner with popular local stores and restaurants to offer you a wide range of products, delivered directly to your doorstep.

Our main hub is located near Kalamandir Theatre in Chilakaluripet. As an online delivery service, our primary focus is on bringing products to you efficiently.

We are open for orders and deliveries from Monday to Sunday, from 9:00 AM to 9:00 PM.

Ordering & Delivery

You can place an order directly through our website by adding items to your cart from the Groceries or Organics pages, then proceeding to the Order Now page. You can also place special orders from our local partners via the Popular Stores page.

We offer delivery within a 10km radius of our hub in Chilakaluripet. If your location is outside this range, you may be prompted to select our "Pick Up" option instead.

Yes, you can select "Pick Up" as your order type on the Order Now page and collect your order directly from our hub during operating hours.

After placing your order, you will receive an order ID. You can track your order status on our Track Order page using this ID.
